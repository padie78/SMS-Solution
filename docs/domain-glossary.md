# Sustainability & ESG Glossary

- **Scope 1 (Direct):** Emissions from sources owned or controlled by the company (e.g., burning diesel in a generator).
- **Scope 2 (Indirect):** Emissions from the generation of purchased energy (e.g., electricity bill from the grid).
- **Scope 3 (Value Chain):** All other indirect emissions (e.g., employee travel, waste, supply chain).
- **Emission Factor (EF):** A representative value that attempts to relate the quantity of a pollutant released to the atmosphere with an activity associated with the release (kg CO2e / unit).
- **CO2e (Carbon Dioxide Equivalent):** A metric measure used to compare the emissions from various greenhouse gases on the basis of their global-warming potential (GWP).
- **Energy Intensity:** Energy consumption divided by a functional unit (e.g., kWh per m2, or kWh per produced item).
- **MACC (Marginal Abatement Cost Curve):** A graph used to visualize the cost-effectiveness of different carbon reduction options.
